**User**: "How do I check if a company's VAT number is valid?"
**Assistant Response**: "You can check EU VAT numbers through the VIES database (https://ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/vies/), but it requires manual lookup. **User**: "What's the risk of trading with fake suppliers?"
**Assistant Response**: "Risk is high: you could face fines up to 10% of transaction value, plus legal liability.
